Car Stereo

Tech Audio's skilled team, with over 35 years of experience, can rise to any occasion when it comes to a car stereo project. Your car audio needs will be met to satisfaction by our team every time. Whether it's a simple Bluetooth radio installation or a custom subwoofer enclosure, our team is able to meet your needs at your price point. The service menu below does not include prices because of the unique nature of every vehicle. Please use the Request a Quote form to get more information about any of the services you see here.

Marine Audio

We can work on your boat stereo here at our shop or we are authorized to come right to your slip to do the job. We are fully insured, so your boat is protected while we're doing your boat audio installation. Every job is custom and you and your project will be treated as such while you are a Tech Audio client.

Window Tint

Window tint offers privacy, security, protection from glare and U.V. rays. Automotive window tinting at Tech Audio can be scheduled by calling 616.844.1747. Tech Audio's professional installers have more than 47 years of experience in window tinting. We offer a lifetime warranty on our Llumar window tint film.
